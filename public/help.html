Frequently Asked Questions

You can change or cancel your flight booking through the 'Manage Booking' section on our website or mobile app. Simply enter your booking reference and last name to access your reservation. Depending on your fare type, changes or cancellations may be subject to fees.

For immediate assistance, you can also call our customer service at +1 (800) 777-3999.

Baggage allowance depends on your travel class and Skywards membership tier. Here's a general guide:

  • First Class: 50kg (110lb)
  • Business Class: 40kg (88lb)
  • Economy Class: 30kg (66lb)

Silver Skywards members receive an additional 12kg, Gold members 16kg, and Platinum members 20kg.

Online check-in opens 48 hours before departure and closes 90 minutes before departure for Economy Class (60 minutes for Business and First Class). To check in:

  1. Visit our website or open the Emirates app
  2. Select 'Check-in'
  3. Enter your booking reference and last name
  4. Follow the prompts to select seats and receive your boarding pass

Requirements vary by destination but typically include:

  • A valid passport (with at least 6 months validity)
  • Any required visas
  • COVID-19 vaccination certificates (where applicable)
  • Completed entry forms for your destination country

Use our Travel Requirements tool for destination-specific information.

You can earn Skywards miles in several ways:

  • Flying with Emirates or our partner airlines
  • Using partner hotels and car rental services
  • Purchasing through our shopping portal
  • Using an Emirates co-branded credit card
  • Participating in our dining partners program

The number of miles earned depends on your fare class and Skywards tier.
